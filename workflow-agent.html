AI Workflow Discovery Agent
Find where AI can help your business.

A system that interviews a business about how it operates, identifies the tasks where AI agents could save the most time, and produces a prioritized report with estimated productivity gains and implementation complexity — before a single line of automation code is written.

Python LLM Workflow Analysis AI Agents Automation SaaS

The problem it solves

🔍

Most businesses don't know where to start with AI

Companies hear about automation but have no structured way to figure out which of their own processes would actually benefit — and which wouldn't be worth the effort.

📊

Consultants are expensive and slow

A traditional workflow audit takes weeks and costs thousands. This system can produce an equivalent discovery report in a single session, at a fraction of the cost.

⚡

Prioritization before implementation

Every identified opportunity is scored by estimated productivity gain and implementation complexity — so decision-makers can act on the highest-value items first.

How it works

01

Workflow Interview

An AI agent guides the user through a structured conversation about their business processes — what tasks exist, who does them, how long they take, and what tools are involved.

02

Opportunity Detection

The system analyzes the workflow map and flags tasks that are repetitive, rule-based, data-heavy, or time-consuming — the classic candidates for AI automation.

03

Complexity & Gain Estimation

Each opportunity is scored: how much time could be saved per week, how complex it would be to automate, and what type of AI agent would be required.

04

Prioritized Report

The output is a structured report ranking automation opportunities by ROI — ready to hand to a developer or use as the basis for a product roadmap.
